Privacy

This site sets no cookies and collects no personal data. This page details that, in compliance with the GDPR.

Cookies

No cookies are set on your browser when visiting this site. No consent banner is required.

Personal data collected by the editor

The site editor collects no personal data. No forms, no third-party analytics tools (Google Analytics, Plausible, etc.), no advertising trackers, no social pixels are deployed.

Hosting provider technical logs

The hosting provider, Cloudflare, Inc., may record technical information necessary for the security and performance of the service: IP address, browser type, page accessed, date and time. These logs are processed under Cloudflare's privacy policy: cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

The site editor has no access to these logs beyond the aggregated and anonymized data provided by default in their Cloudflare dashboard.

Your rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (EU 2016/679), you have rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection regarding data concerning you.

Since the editor collects no personal data, these rights are moot toward the editor. For hosting-level technical logs, these rights are exercised directly with Cloudflare via the contact details in their policy.

Subprocessors

The site uses a single technical provider: Cloudflare (hosting, CDN, DNS). No other provider has access to requests made on this site.
